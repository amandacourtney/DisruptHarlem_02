About Me

Born and raised in The Bougie Down Bronx. Street culture is a heavy factor in why I am interested in certain creative fields. Living in a borough surrounded by lack of resources art never seemed like a valid option to make a living. You always hear that creative fields are the least secure and financially unstable.

By creating an exclusive social media interaction platform; the goal is to increase entrepreneurial skill sets of emerging artist so that they are able to generate income through freelancing and other available job opportunities they are unaware about.
