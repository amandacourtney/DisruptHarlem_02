Money Woe's

Your art fuels you, but it also has the capability to support you. Would you like to find out how?

5 questions to ask yourself

1. What frustrates you the most about being an emerging artist?

2. In what ways have you tried to solve these frustrations that you are having?

3. How often do you find yourself working odd jobs that drain your ambition and what are they?

4. Name a few ways you have tried to promote your art.

5. What are you willing to do to gain the skills required for you to become a freelancer?
